Xian Leaderboard (Trades & Liquidity via con_pairs)

Scoring Breakdown

Bridging (con_usdc.mint): 1 point per 100 USDC minted (capped at 5 points). Points go to kwargs.to.

Trading & Liquidity: We read Swap & Mint events from con_pairs, where token1=currency (1 currency = 0.01 USDC).

  • Swap (Swap event): 1 point / 10 USDC eq, min 10 USDC eq, capped at 50 points per swap. Award points to event.signer.
  • Mint (Mint event): 1 point / 10 USDC eq added, min 10 USDC eq, capped at 100 points. Also awarded to event.signer.

Other Fixed Tasks:

  • NFT Mint (con_pixel_frames.create_thing): +5
  • NFT Purchase (con_pixel_frames.buy_thing): +3
  • XNS Mint (con_name_service_final.mint_name): +2
  • Xian Token Transfer (currency.transfer): +1 (must transfer ≥ 1 token)

Note: Contract deployment points have been removed. If evt.signer is always con_dex_v2 in your chain logs, you may need additional logic to trace the real user.
