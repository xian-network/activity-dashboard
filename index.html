Xian Leaderboard (All Tasks + Sybil Checks)

Volume-Based Tasks

We award points based on volume only for the following actions, and only if they involve con_usdc or currency. We count 1 currency = 0.01 USDC.

  • Bridging (con_usdc/mint): 1 point per 10 USDC minted, capped at 100 pts per transaction, and requires at least 10 USDC minted. Points go to kwargs.to (the bridged user).
  • Swapping (con_dex_v2/swapExactTokenForTokenSupportingFeeOnTransferTokens): 1 point per 10 USDC eq swapped (kwargs.amountIn), capped at 50 pts, minimum 10 USDC eq to qualify. Points awarded to the transaction sender.
  • Add Liquidity (con_dex_v2/addLiquidity): sum the USDC eq for tokenA + tokenB, 1 point per 10 USDC eq, capped at 100 pts, min 10 USDC eq total to qualify. Points awarded to the sender.

Fixed-Point Tasks

  • NFT Mint (con_pixel_frames/create_thing): +5 pts
  • NFT Purchase (con_pixel_frames/buy_thing): +3 pts
  • XNS Mint (con_name_service_final/mint_name): +2 pts
  • Xian Token Transfer (currency/transfer): +1 pt (requires at least 1 token transferred to get points)
  • Contract Deployment (submission/submit_contract): +15 pts (only once per unique contract code – repeated codes yield 0).

Sybil Resistance

We apply several filters to prevent trivial or spammy transactions:

  • Minimum Volume for bridging, swapping, addLiquidity (10 USDC eq)
  • Xian Transfer Threshold: must transfer at least 1 token to get the +1 point
  • Unique Contract Code: only first submission of a given code is scored
  • Per-TX Caps: bridging max 100 pts, swapping max 50 pts, addLiquidity max 100 pts

This ensures the leaderboard reflects meaningful usage rather than repeated micro transactions or duplicate contract submissions.
