Xian Activity Dashboard (Net Liquidity)

Scoring Breakdown

Bridging (con_usdc.mint): 1 point per 100 USDC minted (capped at 5 points). Points go to kwargs.to.

Swaps (con_pairs Swap event): 1 point per 10 USDC eq (min 10, capped at 50) awarded immediately. We assume pair=1 is con_usdc & currency.

Liquidity (Mint & Burn events in con_pairs): we don’t award points per transaction. Instead, we track the user’s net minted minus net burned. The final net is converted to points at 1 point per 10 USDC eq (capped at 100). If a user adds and later removes the same amount of liquidity, their net is zero → no final liquidity points. This prevents repeated add–remove cycles.
